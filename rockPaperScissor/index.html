Rock Paper Scissors

Rules to Play, How?


Our Childhood hand game rock-paper-scissors is a game played by us to enjoy our time. It is stated that it is also
used to clear disputes between persons.
This game is played in a series of rounds and the one who wins most rounds wins the game.


All you need to know is:
-Rock(👊) beats scissors(✌)
-Scissors(✌) beats paper(🖐)
-Paper(🖐) beats rock(👊)
